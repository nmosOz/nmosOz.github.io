Background:

I am from Baldwinsville, NY and have lived there my whole life. When I was in 8th grade I took a robotics class and have loved programming ever since. I graduated from SUNY OCC in May of 2022 and went on to get my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science from SUNY Oswego and graduated in May of 2024.

In college I was part of the Computer Science Club and the Math Club. I liked doing work in the computer science lounge as I could collaborate with fellow students as well as help others that needed it.

My strongest languages are SQL (including mySQL and other SQL plugins in Python), Java and Python. However, I have made projects with many others including C, Go, Clojure, and OCaml. I love learning new programming languages and new things that are possible within the languages I already know!
